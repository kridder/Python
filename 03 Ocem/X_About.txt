Ocem (working title)

A math game using the octal system and custom symbols to represent the numbers.
See X_Ref.jpg to see what number corresponds with what symbol.

Press 'p' to start play mode.
Use the mouse to enter the correct answer to the displayed math question by using the mouse.
You can use the number buttons at the bottom of the screen or mouse over the greyed out answer and click the desired position.
Then click enter to check you answer and then click enter again to go to the next question.
